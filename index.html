Elon Musk

Facts about Elon Musk

  • 1971 - Born on June 28th in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1995 - Co-founds Zip2, a software company
  • 1999 - Sells Zip2 for $307 million
  • 1999 - Co-founds X.com, which later becomes PayPal
  • 2002 - PayPal is acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion
  • 2002 - Establishes SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corp.)
  • 2004 - Invests in Tesla Motors and becomes chairman of the board
  • 2006 - Co-founds Tesla, Inc. and unveils the Tesla Roadster
  • 2008 - SpaceX's Falcon 1 becomes the first privately-funded liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit
  • 2010 - SpaceX successfully launches and recovers the Dragon spacecraft
  • 2012 - Tesla introduces the Model S electric sedan
  • 2015 - SpaceX successfully lands a Falcon 9 rocket on a drone ship
  • 2016 - Tesla announces the acquisition of SolarCity
  • 2017 - SpaceX launches the Falcon Heavy, the most powerful operational rocket in the world
  • 2020 - SpaceX sends astronauts to the International Space Station (ISS) in the Crew Dragon spacecraft
  • 2021 - Tesla becomes the world's most valuable car company by market capitalization
  • 2021 - Tesla Cybertruck and Tesla Bot announced
  • 2021 - Musk becomes the richest person in the world, according to Forbes
